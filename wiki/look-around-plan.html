LookAround Enhancement Plan

Kitty Barrage · May 2026

Use cases to support

Curious kitty

Looks left and right quickly, multiple times. Short pauses. High energy.

speed=1.8 · reps=3 · pause_left=0.1 · pause_right=0.1
Cautious kitty

Looks left slowly, holds there a long time, then continues on its way without looking right at all.

speed=0.6 · reps=1 · left_only=true · pause_left=2.0
Lazy glance

One slow look both ways, long pause facing forward at the end before returning to the brain loop.

speed=0.7 · reps=1 · pause_center=1.5
Paranoid kitty (loop)

Loops continuously looking both ways fast until interrupted by movement.

speed=2.0 · loop=true · pause_left=0 · pause_right=0

Current phase sequence

center → right→ right → left→ left → center

With pauses and direction flags, the full proposed sequence becomes:

center → right→ pause right→ right → left→ pause left→ left → center→ pause center→ repeat / exit

Dashed = optional (skipped if left_only, right_only, or pause = 0). Pause phases run in tick(delta) — no animation, just a timer counting down before moving to the next phase.

Layout idea

The button column would expand for Look Around with a collapsible sub-panel, or the controls appear inline below the button row. Sliders show current value as a label.

All values read at button-press time — not live-applied. So you can set params, then press Look Around to test that exact configuration.
